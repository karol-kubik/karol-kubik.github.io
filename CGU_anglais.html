Description of the website

Objectives : This website is the showcase of the startup AEROPEX. It has two main objectives. The first one is to indicate to any visitor wishing to know more about the company AEROPEX and its employer INFINITE MEASURES what our work consists in. They will then be able to find out how to take the psycho-technical tests, as well as their general content, in order to understand the psychological and psycho-motor skills required to become a fighter pilot.
The second objective is to make the link between the students, potential future fighter pilots, and their instructors. Through an identification on the site, they will have access to a profile that will allow them several actions depending on their status. Students will be able to access their results. Instructors will be able to enter the results of their students, and consult all of these results at any time. Finally, the administrators will have access to all the data of the site, in order to manage the potential instabilities and problems encountered.

Rights and obligations of the user

The rights of the user vary according to his status. This site has four statuses: visitor, student, instructor and administrator.

Status 1: Visitor
The visitor has no access to any sensitive data on the site. Only the public pages are authorized to him. He can however create a new account via the "register" tab. If his new account is validated by an administrator, he changes his status and becomes a "student".

Status 2: Student
The student has an account on the site. After identification via the "login" tab, he/she can access his/her private space listing all his/her personal information, as well as the results of the tests he/she may have taken, if these have been published by his/her instructor. He cannot change his status.

Status 3 : Instructor
The instructor has an account on the site. After identification via the "login" tab, he can access his private space, and thus access all the data he has entered, as well as all his students' accounts. He can only modify their test results; their personal information remains unchangeable. .

Status 4: Administrator
The administrator has an account on the site. After logging in via the "log in" tab, he/she can access his/her private area, and thus all his/her personal data and those of the entire site. Only the passwords of students and instructors are not accessible to him. The administrator is also the editor, and his rights and obligations are not limited to those presented here (cf "Rights and obligations of the editor")
